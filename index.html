Meet Lisa:

After spending years in Hollywood CA, as a successful television make-up artist, Lisa moved to Brooklyn, NY with her husband and young daughter Autumn.

Currently she is a NY State Licensed make-up instructor working at Make-Up Designory, the premier school for beauty and special fx make-up

Lisa found her love for face painting after attending a fundraiser at her daughter's school. While on a giant line awaiting face paint by overworked moms, Lisa decided to pitch in and quickly discovered two things. The first that doing face paint is harder than it looks! The second that it was going to be her new passion.

A year and a lot of practice later, Lisa has created Artsy Face Painting, a face painting service specializing in parties, fundraisers, festivals and corporate events.
